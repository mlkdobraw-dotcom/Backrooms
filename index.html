LEVEL 0

THE LOBBY

Level 0 is the first level
of the Backrooms.
It appears as an infinite maze
of empty, windowless rooms
with yellow walls and a
musty, stale atmosphere.

☷ OVERVIEW
  • Difficulty: Safe
  • Class: 0
  • Habitat: Indoor
  • Survival Rate: High
  • Entities: None known
  • Exits: Unknown
◉ APPEARANCE

Level 0 is composed of endless, randomly generated rooms and corridors.

The walls are covered in old, yellowish wallpaper, the floor is moist carpet, and the ceiling is filled with buzzing fluorescent lights.

⚙ CHARACTERISTICS
  • Endless and maze-like
  • No windows or natural light
  • Constant buzzing of fluorescent lights
  • The air is stale and slightly humid
  • No signs of life or movement
⚠ DANGERS

Level 0 is generally considered safe. However, the real danger is the psychological effect of being trapped in an infinite, empty place with no way out.

💡 TIPS
  • Stay calm and keep moving.
  • Try to find an exit, though they are extremely rare.
  • Avoid noclipping further into the unknown.

LEVEL FUN

THE PARTY

Level Fun appears as a colorful
and playful place, but behind
the smiles and balloons lies
one of the most dangerous levels
in the Backrooms.

☷OVERVIEW
  • Difficulty: Very High
  • Class: 5
  • Habitat: Indoor
  • Survival Rate: Very Low
  • Entities: Partygoers
  • Exits: Rare
◉APPEARANCE

Level Fun is filled with bright colors, balloons, banners, and confetti. The walls are covered in childish drawings and messages like "FUN!" and "COME JOIN THE PARTY!".

The lights flicker sometimes, and cheerful music can be heard in the distance.

⚙CHARACTERISTICS
  • Colorful and festive environment
  • Balloons, confetti, and party decorations everywhere
  • Cheery music and distant laughter
  • Infinite maze-like layout
  • Some areas may look slightly different, but never the same
⚠DANGERS
  • Home to the Partygoers, who will try to make you join their party
  • Being caught by a Partygoer can result in being turned into one of them
  • Disorienting layout can lead to endless wandering
  • False sense of safety due to its playful look
💡TIPS
  • Do not trust the Partygoers
  • Avoid eye contact and keep moving
  • Stay quiet and stick to the walls
  • Finding an exit is extremely rare
  • Do not stay too long in one place

THE POOLROOMS

LEVEL 37

Level 37, also known as The
Poolrooms, is a level composed
of infinite, surreal pools and
tiled rooms. The water is
deep, the silence is heavy,
and something moves beneath
the surface.

☷OVERVIEW
  • Difficulty: High
  • Class: 3
  • Habitat: Indoor / Aquatic
  • Survival Rate: Low
  • Entities: Several
  • Exits: Uncommon
◉APPEARANCE

The Poolrooms are made up of vast, tiled spaces filled with deep, clear water. The architecture is surreal and inconsistent, with long hallways, staircases that descend into the water, and rooms that seem to stretch forever.

The lighting is dim and hazy, often reflected by the water's surface.

⚙CHARACTERISTICS
  • Endless, maze-like rooms filled with water
  • Water is usually calm, but depth is deceptive
  • Echoes travel far and are hard to locate
  • Temperature is cold and increases fatigue
  • Some rooms are completely dark or partially submerged
⚠DANGERS
  • Drowning is the most common cause of death
  • Unknown aquatic entities can attack from below
  • Getting lost in the endless layout can lead to exhaustion or madness
  • Some water may be deeper than it looks
  • Limited safe areas to rest
💡TIPS
  • Stay near walls and keep track of turns
  • Avoid entering deep water if possible
  • Listen for sounds behind you
  • Conserve energy and avoid unnecessary swimming
  • Look for dry platforms or rooms to rest

ENTITY #3

SMILER

😀 IT ALWAYS SMILES

The Smiler is one of the most
feared entities in the Backrooms.
It hides in the darkness, visible
only by its wide, glowing smile.
By the time you see it —
it is already too late.

☷FILE
  • Threat Level: Extreme
  • Type: Predatory
  • Habitat: Dark areas
  • Speed: Very Fast
  • Size: Human-like
  • Status: Active
◉APPEARANCE

The Smiler appears as a dark, humanoid silhouette lurking in unlit corners. Its only visible feature is a wide, tooth-filled grin that glows faintly in the dark.

Some survivors report that it seems to mimic human posture, standing completely still until it decides to move.

⚙BEHAVIOR
  • Lurks in dark corners and unlit hallways
  • Does not move while being observed
  • Attacks the moment you look away
  • Appears to enjoy the fear of its prey
  • Can appear in groups in deeper levels
⚠DANGERS
  • Attacks with extreme speed once you break eye contact
  • Darkness is its domain — it has full advantage
  • No known way to kill or repel it
  • Screaming or panicking attracts more Smilers
💡SURVIVAL TIPS
  • Never enter an unlit area without a light source
  • If you see a smile in the dark — do not run yet
  • Back away slowly while maintaining eye contact
  • Find a lit area as fast as possible
  • Do not travel alone in low-light levels
⚠REALITY IS AN ILLUSION. THE BACKROOMS ARE REAL.

ENTITY #8

HOUND

🙉 YOU WON'T HEAR IT COMING

The Hound is a fast, aggressive
predator that roams many levels
of the Backrooms. It moves on
all fours with terrifying speed
and hunts primarily by sound
and movement.

☷FILE
  • Threat Level: High
  • Type: Feral / Predatory
  • Habitat: Multiple levels
  • Speed: Extremely Fast
  • Size: Large dog-like
  • Status: Active
◉APPEARANCE

The Hound resembles a large, hairless creature that moves on all fours. Its skin is dark and leathery, its eyes glow a deep orange-red, and its mouth is filled with uneven, sharp teeth.

It makes little to no sound while moving, making it nearly impossible to hear before it strikes.

⚙BEHAVIOR
  • Hunts using sound — loud noises will attract it instantly
  • Moves in unpredictable, erratic patterns
  • Will chase prey relentlessly once locked on
  • Sometimes found in packs on dangerous levels
  • Territorial — it will patrol the same corridors repeatedly
⚠DANGERS
  • One of the fastest entities in the Backrooms
  • Nearly silent movement gives no warning before attack
  • Outrunning it is nearly impossible
  • Cornering yourself while fleeing is a common fatal mistake
💡SURVIVAL TIPS
  • Move slowly and quietly at all times
  • Avoid running — vibrations and sound attract it
  • If spotted, throw an object in the opposite direction
  • Hide in small spaces it cannot enter
  • Never dead-end yourself in a corridor

ENTITY #17

SKIN-STEALER

👤 IT WEARS YOU

The Skin-Stealer is among the
most disturbing entities known.
It mimics human form perfectly,
wearing the appearance of those
it has taken. You may not know
it is there — until it is you.

☷FILE
  • Threat Level: Extreme
  • Type: Mimic / Predatory
  • Habitat: All levels
  • Speed: Variable
  • Size: Human-sized
  • Status: Active
◉APPEARANCE

The Skin-Stealer appears as a tall, pale humanoid with elongated limbs and a blank, featureless face — in its natural state. However, it can perfectly mimic the appearance of any human it has encountered.

Up close, signs of its true nature emerge: slightly wrong proportions, delayed blinking, or an unnatural stillness.

⚙BEHAVIOR
  • Mimics humans to approach without raising alarm
  • Observes from a distance before closing in
  • Rarely attacks openly — prefers isolation and deception
  • Can replicate voice and mannerisms of known people
  • Will not break its disguise unless cornered
⚠DANGERS
  • Nearly impossible to identify in disguise
  • Lures survivors into isolation before striking
  • May have already replaced someone in your group
  • Creates paranoia and distrust among survivors
💡SURVIVAL TIPS
  • Establish a code word known only to your group
  • Watch for subtle wrongness: blinking, posture, timing
  • Never go anywhere alone with someone you just met
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels off, it is
  • Do not let it separate you from others
