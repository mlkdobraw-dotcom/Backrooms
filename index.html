LEVEL 0

THE LOBBY

Level 0 is the first level
of the Backrooms.
It appears as an infinite maze
of empty, windowless rooms
with yellow walls and a
musty, stale atmosphere.

☷ OVERVIEW
  • Difficulty: Safe
  • Class: 0
  • Habitat: Indoor
  • Survival Rate: High
  • Entities: None known
  • Exits: Unknown
◉ APPEARANCE

Level 0 is composed of endless, randomly generated rooms and corridors.

The walls are covered in old, yellowish wallpaper, the floor is moist carpet, and the ceiling is filled with buzzing fluorescent lights.

⚙ CHARACTERISTICS
  • Endless and maze-like
  • No windows or natural light
  • Constant buzzing of fluorescent lights
  • The air is stale and slightly humid
  • No signs of life or movement
⚠ DANGERS

Level 0 is generally considered safe. However, the real danger is the psychological effect of being trapped in an infinite, empty place with no way out.

💡 TIPS
  • Stay calm and keep moving.
  • Try to find an exit, though they are extremely rare.
  • Avoid noclipping further into the unknown.
⚠ REALITY IS AN ILLUSION. THE BACKROOMS ARE REAL.

LEVEL FUN

THE PARTY

Level Fun appears as a colorful
and playful place, but behind
the smiles and balloons lies
one of the most dangerous levels
in the Backrooms.

☷OVERVIEW
  • Difficulty: Very High
  • Class: 5
  • Habitat: Indoor
  • Survival Rate: Very Low
  • Entities: Partygoers
  • Exits: Rare
◉APPEARANCE

Level Fun is filled with bright colors, balloons, banners, and confetti. The walls are covered in childish drawings and messages like "FUN!" and "COME JOIN THE PARTY!".

The lights flicker sometimes, and cheerful music can be heard in the distance.

⚙CHARACTERISTICS
  • Colorful and festive environment
  • Balloons, confetti, and party decorations everywhere
  • Cheery music and distant laughter
  • Infinite maze-like layout
  • Some areas may look slightly different, but never the same
⚠DANGERS
  • Home to the Partygoers, who will try to make you join their party
  • Being caught by a Partygoer can result in being turned into one of them
  • Disorienting layout can lead to endless wandering
  • False sense of safety due to its playful look
💡TIPS
  • Do not trust the Partygoers
  • Avoid eye contact and keep moving
  • Stay quiet and stick to the walls
  • Finding an exit is extremely rare
  • Do not stay too long in one place
⚠REALITY IS AN ILLUSION. THE BACKROOMS ARE REAL.

THE POOLROOMS

LEVEL 37

Level 37, also known as The
Poolrooms, is a level composed
of infinite, surreal pools and
tiled rooms. The water is
deep, the silence is heavy,
and something moves beneath
the surface.

☷OVERVIEW
  • Difficulty: High
  • Class: 3
  • Habitat: Indoor / Aquatic
  • Survival Rate: Low
  • Entities: Several
  • Exits: Uncommon
◉APPEARANCE

The Poolrooms are made up of vast, tiled spaces filled with deep, clear water. The architecture is surreal and inconsistent, with long hallways, staircases that descend into the water, and rooms that seem to stretch forever.

The lighting is dim and hazy, often reflected by the water's surface.

⚙CHARACTERISTICS
  • Endless, maze-like rooms filled with water
  • Water is usually calm, but depth is deceptive
  • Echoes travel far and are hard to locate
  • Temperature is cold and increases fatigue
  • Some rooms are completely dark or partially submerged
⚠DANGERS
  • Drowning is the most common cause of death
  • Unknown aquatic entities can attack from below
  • Getting lost in the endless layout can lead to exhaustion or madness
  • Some water may be deeper than it looks
  • Limited safe areas to rest
💡TIPS
  • Stay near walls and keep track of turns
  • Avoid entering deep water if possible
  • Listen for sounds behind you
  • Conserve energy and avoid unnecessary swimming
  • Look for dry platforms or rooms to rest
